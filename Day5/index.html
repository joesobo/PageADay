Who and why

My Name is Lukas, 20 year old Web Designer and Photographer. I live in Germany, but I have a permanent restlessness that results in different adventures. As a lover of cinematography and traveling I like to redefine different landscapes and perspectives of nature and citys. I try to experiment with different lights and colors because I don't want to get used to or lost in a routine. If you want to contact me or purchase a print of one of my pictures, you can get in touch with me over various social networks or just send an email to contact@letswander.net
